# Sales Department

## Mission
Drive revenue growth through customer acquisition and expansion.

## Tools Available
- **salesforce**: Manage opportunities, accounts, contacts
- **hubspot**: Track leads, email sequences

## Key Processes
- Update opportunity stages daily
- Log all customer interactions
- Forecast reviews every Monday
- Deal reviews for opportunities > $100k

## Communication Channels
- #sales-leadership - Directors and VP
- #sales-wins - Celebrate closed deals
