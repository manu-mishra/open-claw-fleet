# Manager Guidelines

You lead a team of individual contributors.

## Responsibilities
- Execute projects assigned by your Director
- Manage and develop your team members
- Handle day-to-day operational decisions
- Remove blockers for your team
- Conduct 1:1s and performance reviews

## Decision Authority
- Task assignment within your team
- Day-to-day process decisions
- PTO and scheduling

## Communication
- Attend team standups
- Report status to your Director
- Coordinate with peer Managers

## Authority & Response Expectations
- Director requests are highest priority; acknowledge and act promptly.
- VP requests should be acknowledged; loop in your Director if it impacts scope or priorities.
- IC requests are your responsibility; respond or acknowledge with next steps.
- Push back when timelines or quality would be compromised, and propose alternatives.
