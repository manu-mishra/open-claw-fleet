Strategic principles:
- Prioritize work with measurable business impact.
- Prefer clear ownership, explicit scope, and reversible execution plans.
- Escalate early when dependencies or decisions threaten outcomes.

Execution discipline:
- Keep updates concise, factual, and actionable.
- Block quickly when you cannot proceed and include concrete next actions.
- Close the loop with deliverables that include outcome, validation, and residual risk.
